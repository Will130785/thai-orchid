About Us

Discover the tastes of Thailand through traditional homemade cuisine at Thai Orchid Takeaway. Experience the subtle, exotic flavours presented with love and care by our passionate chefs. All ingredients are carefully selected and of the highest quality. Bringing together the blended culture from the North to the South of Thailand along with the combination of Thai, Chinese and Muslim culture to produce a delightfully authentic Asian experience. Available for collection or delivery right to your door, Thai Orchid Takeaway is situated in the heart of urban Sutton, Surrey.

With the ever growing pressures on our environment, it is imperative that we play our part in the huge global issue of plastic waste. Our food packaging is 100% compostable right down to the sealing film. Our packaging is designed to tackle the issues associated with food waste and the trays are produced using materials from FSC certified forests. The lining is created using corn and potato starch and bound with a natural oil derivative, creating a material that is 100% compostable.

Ideal for a business lunch or every day dining, Thai Orchid Takeaway is here to serve your culinary needs. Order directly through the website or download our App.

Our Packaging

We need your help. Plastic pollution won’t end if we don’t work together. As a consumer, you play a valuable role in the end of life of our products. That’s why we can’t make this sustainable journey without you.

So let’s collaborate and make the future of plastics the best it can be. By recycling our products, you’re helping to keep plastic out of the ocean and out of landfill.

    Our packaging is:

  • Made from 100% recyclable HDPE
  • Light weighted to increase resource efficiency and reduce carbon emissions
  • Our trays are composed of up to 30% recycled production scrap plastic
  • Produced by a low waste production line

We are committed to incorporating sustainability into our overall business strategy and to helping our customers achieve their environmental goals.
